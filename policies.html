Academic Norms

Members are expected to maintain respectful academic discussion standards and follow the Columbia Code of Conduct.

The group is currently informal and under active development. Reading schedules, seminars, notes, and project structures may evolve over time.

Members may leave at any time if the reading group is not useful or interesting to them. We appreciate everyone’s presence and contribution.
