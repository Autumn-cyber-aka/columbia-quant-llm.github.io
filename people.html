Community Background

The community currently includes members with experience at Jump Trading, JQ Investments, DeepMind, Meta, AlphaGrep, Google, Citadel Securities, Kimi / Moonshot AI, Qwen, and Tencent Hunyuan. This includes three current or incoming Jump Trading interns, two JQ Investments interns, one full-time DeepMind researcher, and additional current interns, incoming interns, and full-time researchers across the listed organizations.

The community also includes contributors from Penn CS PhD programs and members of the MathHeart community.

Members are encouraged to introduce themselves and share work related to LLMs, learning theory, post-training, verification, reasoning, AI systems, OPD, post-training harnesses, or quantitative research.

Open Contributors

Contributors may help with reading notes, paper selection, website maintenance, replication code, seminar organization, and research discussions.

To be listed on the course page, send your GitHub account and a short research direction to t.zhang8@columbia.edu.
